#Imperfect

Drop the -ons ending from the present nous form of the verb, then add these endings: 

<div class="conjugation-table">
<h3>-er, -ir, and -re</h3>
<table>
  <tr>
    <td>-ais</td>
    <td>-ions</td> 
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>-ais</td>
    <td>-iez</td> 
  </tr>
  <tr>
    <td>-ait</td>
    <td>-aient</td> 
  </tr>
</table>
</div>
